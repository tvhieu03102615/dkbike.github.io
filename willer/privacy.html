Privacy policy

We call the WILLER GROUP MISSION to contribute to the realization of a convenient society by creating a new infrastructure for the movement of people in Japan.
Based on this philosophy, as a basic policy, continue to create new things that can be enjoyed by each person, provide the best service to customers, practice fair business activities and raise corporate value We strive to.
We believe that it is the responsibility of the business operator and social responsibility how to safely handle the personal information of customers and other people related to our company in order to realize such a philosophy.
In order to earn the trust of our customers, we have established a personal information protection policy and will actively work on the appropriate protection of personal information.

1. Efforts to protect personal information
The Willer Group will work on this matter with the entire company, based on the recognition that "Personal information protection is one of the most important business and business issues of the Group.
2. Appropriate collection, use and provision of personal information
Handling of collection, use, provision, etc. of personal information is carried out by lawful and structured means, and it is not used except for the purpose of use, and the range of use is limited and handled appropriately.
3. Providing personal information to third parties
The Group will not disclose or provide it to third parties without your prior consent, except for legal requests.
In addition, when the customer's personal information is provided to the business consignee, we require the business consignee to protect the personal information by confidentiality agreement etc. and manage the business consignee to handle the personal information appropriately.
4. Protection of personal data held
We formulate and clarify management rules such as collection, use, provision and deposit of information about all personal information handled in our group, and perform appropriate management according to the actual situation of our business. In order to prevent loss, destruction, falsification and leakage of personal information, we will carry out information security measures such as unauthorized access measures and anti-virus measures.
5. Compliance with laws and regulations
We will comply with the laws, guidelines, social norms, and public order and customs related to the Personal Information Protection Law.
6. Education and training
In addition to raising awareness on personal information protection for executives and employees, the Group provides education / training on personal information.
7. Accident prevention and response
The Group strives to prevent personal information leaks and other incidents, and promptly takes appropriate measures, including measures to prevent recurrence, should an accident occur.
